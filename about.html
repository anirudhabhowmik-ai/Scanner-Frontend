Our Story

NextoPDF was born from a simple frustration: why should you need to pay a subscription just to merge two PDF files? Why should you upload your private documents to a server just to convert them?

We built NextoPDF to answer these questions. Using modern web technologies, we created a suite of PDF tools that run entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device. You never see a paywall. You never hit a usage limit.

What started as a single PDF merger has grown into a comprehensive toolkit used by thousands of people every day. Students working on assignments. Professionals preparing reports. Anyone who needs quick, reliable PDF tools without the hassle.
