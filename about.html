The Story Behind NextoPDF

My name is Anirudha, and I built NextoPDF because I was frustrated. I had a 45MB portfolio PDF for a job application, and every job site rejected it. "File too large." "Maximum 10MB." I spent hours looking for a tool that would shrink it - everything either wanted my credit card or added ugly watermarks.

That 45MB file became 8MB using a tool I built myself in a weekend. I shared it with a few friends. One was a lawyer with 40 boxes of old contracts who needed to make them searchable. Another was a baker who couldn't post her menu on Instagram because it was a PDF. A realtor needed to send listing brochures without email bouncing back.

Their problems became new tools. OCR for scanned documents. PDF to images for social media. Compression for email attachments. What started as one tool for my job search grew into a collection used by thousands. No sign-ups. No limits. No data collection. Just tools that work, because I needed them to work too.
