    Adding page numbers to PDF files is essential for professional documents, legal filings, academic papers, and any multi-page document that needs proper organization. Page numbers help readers navigate your document, reference specific sections, and ensure pages are in correct order.     Page Number Format Examples

    • Simple Numbering: "1, 2, 3" (leave custom text empty)
    • Page X of Y: "Page {n} of {total}"
    • Section Numbering: "Section 1 - Page {n}"
    • Chapter Numbering: "Chapter 3 | Page {n}"
    • Appendix Format: "A-{n}" (set start number to 1)
