    Why Add a Watermark to Your PDF Documents?

    Watermarks are essential for protecting your intellectual property, establishing brand identity, and preventing unauthorized distribution. Whether you're a photographer sharing proofs, a business sending confidential proposals, a teacher distributing worksheets, or a designer showcasing portfolios, adding a visible watermark ensures your work remains attributed to you.

    Text Watermarks vs. Image Watermarks

    Text watermarks are ideal for adding words like "Confidential", "Draft", "Sample", "Review Copy", or your company name and website. You can customize font family, size, color (with hex picker), bold/italic/underline styling, and rotation angle. Image watermarks work best for company logos, signatures, stamps, or visual branding elements. Both types support opacity control (from subtle 10% to strong 100%) and precise positioning via drag-and-drop.
