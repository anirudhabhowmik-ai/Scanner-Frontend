Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Application Writer work?
Enter your name, the receiver's details, describe your situation in plain words, and click Generate. The AI automatically detects the type of application — leave, complaint, bank, medical, job, NOC — and writes a complete, polished formal letter in seconds.
Is this AI Application Writer free to use?
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no login required. Just fill in your details and generate instantly.
What types of applications can I generate?
Leave applications, medical leave letters, complaint letters, bank applications, job application letters, NOC requests, fee concession applications, transfer certificate requests, and any other formal application. The AI auto-detects the type from your description — no need to select manually.
Can I write applications in Bengali or Hindi?
Yes! Select Bengali (বাংলা) or Hindi (हिंदी) from the Language dropdown before generating. The entire letter — including formatting and salutations — will be written in your selected language.
Can I edit the generated application?
Yes! The output appears in a rich text editor. You can click anywhere to edit, and use the formatting toolbar to bold, italicize, underline, change font size, color, alignment, and more — just like a word processor.
What download formats are available?
You can download as Plain Text (.txt), Word Document (.doc, opens in Microsoft Word / LibreOffice), or PDF using your browser's built-in print-to-PDF feature. All formatting you applied in the editor is preserved.
