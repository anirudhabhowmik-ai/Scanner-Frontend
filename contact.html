Quick Answers

Is NextoPDF really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees, no subscriptions, and no usage limits. We believe PDF tools should be accessible to everyone.

How do you protect my files?

Your files are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. They never get uploaded to our servers, so we literally cannot access them. Your privacy is built into our technology.

What if I need help?

Email us at support@nextopdf.com or use the contact form. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, mention "urgent" in the subject line.
