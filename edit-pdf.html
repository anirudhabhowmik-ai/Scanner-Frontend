Why Edit PDF Documents Online?

Editing PDF files is essential for making last-minute changes, adding annotations, filling forms, or customizing documents before sharing. Unlike traditional PDF editors that require expensive software installations, our free online PDF editor lets you modify your documents directly in your browser with no registration, no watermarks, and no hidden fees.

✓ 100% Free & Secure: No registration required. No watermark added. All PDF editing happens in your browser where possible, and files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing. Your privacy is our priority.

What You Can Do With Our PDF Editor

  • Add Text Annotations: Insert new text anywhere on any PDF page. Customize font family, size, color, weight, and alignment. Double-click any text to edit it directly on the canvas.
  • Draw Freehand: Use the drawing tool to highlight, underline, or sketch on your PDF. Choose from multiple brush sizes, colors, and styles (solid, dashed, or dotted lines).
  • Insert Shapes: Add rectangles, circles, triangles, and lines to emphasize content or create diagrams. Customize fill color, stroke color, and stroke width.
  • Add Images: Overlay images on your PDF with adjustable opacity and corner radius. Perfect for adding logos, signatures, or visual elements.
  • Extract & Edit Existing Content: Use the "Edit Content" feature to extract existing text and images from your PDF, then reposition, resize, or delete them.
  • Rotate & Reposition: Select any annotation to rotate, resize, or move it anywhere on the page.

How to Use This Online PDF Editor

  1. Upload - Click the upload area and select your PDF file. The tool will load all pages automatically.
  2. Choose a Tool - Select from Draw, Add Text, Shape, Image, or Edit Content from the toolbar.
  3. Customize - Adjust colors, sizes, fonts, and other properties in the property panel that appears.
  4. Apply Annotations - Click or drag on the PDF page to add your text, drawing, shape, or image.
  5. Select & Modify - Click any annotation to select it, then use the floating toolbar to edit, rotate, copy, or delete.
  6. Save - Click "Save PDF" to generate your edited document. Preview and download instantly.

Advanced Features

  • Undo/Redo Support: Made a mistake? Use the Undo button to revert changes or Clear to remove all annotations from the current page.
  • Page Navigation: Use the sidebar thumbnails to switch between pages. Each page maintains its own annotations.
  • Extract & Edit Content: The "Edit Content" feature extracts all existing text blocks and images from your PDF, removes the original content, and places editable versions on the canvas. You can then reposition, resize, or delete any element.
  • Image Corner Radius: Add rounded corners to images for a softer, more professional look.
  • Opacity Control: Adjust the transparency of drawings, text, shapes, and images to create watermarks or subtle annotations.

Common Use Cases for PDF Editing

  • Business Documents: Add signatures, dates, or approval stamps to contracts and agreements.
  • Academic Papers: Highlight key passages, add margin notes, or insert comments on research papers.
  • Forms & Applications: Fill out PDF forms by adding text to empty fields.
  • Design Proofs: Mark up design proofs with annotations, arrows, and notes for clients or team members.
  • Legal Documents: Redact sensitive information by covering it with shapes or deleting content.
  • Presentations: Add visual elements, diagrams, or explanatory text to PDF slides.

Technical Specifications

Our PDF editor uses PDF.js for rendering and Fabric.js for canvas manipulation. Maximum file size: 50MB. Supported PDF versions: 1.4 through 2.0. The tool preserves text layers, vector graphics, embedded fonts, and image quality. Most processing happens in your browser for speed and privacy. When saving, files are processed on our secure servers with TLS encryption and automatically deleted within 60 minutes.

Privacy & Security

Your PDFs are never stored permanently. Files are automatically deleted from our servers within 60 minutes of saving. We do not access, view, or analyze your document content beyond the editing operation. No personal information is collected or required to use this tool.
