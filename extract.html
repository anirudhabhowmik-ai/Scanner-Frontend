    Extraction vs Deletion

    Extraction creates a new PDF, keeping the original safe. Deletion removes pages from the original document permanently. Use extraction for sharing or archiving, and deletion for cleanup.

    Tips for Smooth PDF Page Extraction

    Identify which pages are important and note their PDF page numbers carefully. This is especially important for multi-section extractions.

    Use Ranges Smartly

    Use continuous page ranges for chapters or appendices and combine with individual pages when necessary. This saves time and avoids mistakes.

    Maintain Context

    Include intro, cover, or summary pages if needed to make the extracted PDF understandable on its own.

    Organize and Verify

    Use descriptive file names and verify the extracted PDF page by page to ensure no mistakes or missing content.
