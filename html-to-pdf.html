    Why Convert HTML to PDF Online?

    Converting HTML documents and web pages to PDF format is essential for archiving web content, creating offline documentation, saving receipts, preserving articles, and sharing web pages with others who may not have internet access. Our free HTML to PDF converter captures the exact layout, styles, colors, and formatting of your original content, ensuring professional results every time.

    ✓ 100% Free & Secure: No registration required. No watermark added. All HTML files and URLs are processed in real-time and automatically deleted from our servers after conversion. Your privacy is our priority.

    Two Ways to Convert: URL or File Upload

    • Website URL Conversion: Simply enter any public website URL. Our converter will fetch the page, render it with all styles and images, and generate a high-quality PDF document.
    • HTML File Upload: Upload local HTML files (with .html or .htm extension). Perfect for converting locally saved web pages, email newsletters, or generated HTML reports.
    • Batch Upload: Upload multiple HTML files at once — they will be merged into a single PDF document.

    Customizable Conversion Options

    • Screen Size Simulation: Choose from mobile (320px), tablet (768px), desktop (1440px), or HD (1920px) screen widths to control how the page renders.
    • Page Format: Select "One long page" for continuous scrolling documents or let the converter add page breaks naturally.
    • Margin Control: Choose between no margin, small margin, or big margin for your PDF output.
    • Ad Blocking: Remove common advertisements while preserving header, navigation, and main content.
    • Overlay Removal: Eliminate popup modals, cookie notices, and interstitial overlays that may appear on web pages.
