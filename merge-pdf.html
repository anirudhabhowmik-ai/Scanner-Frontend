    Merge vs. Combine: What's the Difference?

    • Merge PDF: Takes multiple PDF files and combines them sequentially into one document.
    • Combine Pages: Some tools allow selecting specific pages from each PDF to combine.
    • Our Tool: Merges complete PDF files in the order you select. Each file becomes a continuous section in the final document.
