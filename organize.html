    How to Use This PDF Page Organizer

    1. Upload - Click "Choose PDF files" and select one or more PDF documents.
    2. View Pages - All pages from your uploaded files appear as thumbnails in the organizer.
    3. Drag to Reorder - Click and drag any page thumbnail to a new position to reorder pages.
    4. Rotate Pages - Click the rotate button (↻) on any page to rotate it 90 degrees clockwise.
    5. Delete Pages - Click the trash icon to remove unwanted pages.
    6. Add Blank Pages - Click the plus button to insert a blank page after any page.
    7. Save - Click "Organize PDF" to generate your reorganized document and download it.

    Organize vs. Merge vs. Split: Understanding the Differences

    • Organize PDF: Rearrange, rotate, delete, and add pages within a document or across multiple documents.
    • Merge PDF: Combine multiple PDF files into one document (without page-level editing).
    • Split PDF: Divide a single PDF into multiple separate files.
    • Our Tool: Combines organizing capabilities with the ability to work across multiple uploaded files.
