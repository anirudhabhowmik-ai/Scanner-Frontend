    How to Organize PDF Pages (It's Just Drag and Drop)

    1

    Upload Your PDF(s)

    Upload one file or multiple. The tool shows you little thumbnails of every page so you can actually see what you're working with - not just page numbers.

    2

    Drag Pages Around

    Click any page and drag it where you want. Need page 5 to be page 2? Drag it up. Want to move something to the end? Drag it down. You can also rotate pages that are sideways or delete ones you don't need.

    3

    Download the Organized PDF

    Hit download and you're done. Your new PDF has all the pages in exactly the order you arranged. No cutting, no taping, no rescanning.

    üëÅÔ∏è Use the thumbnails, not just page numbers

    I learned this the hard way. Think about what should come first, second, third. Maybe the executive summary should be before the detailed data. Maybe appendices go at the end. A quick mental plan saves dragging things back and forth.

    üîÑ Fix rotation while you're at it

    If some pages are sideways, rotate them right in the same tool. No need to go to a separate tool for that. One and done.

    üóëÔ∏è Delete as you go

    Stuff That Goes Wrong (And How to Fix It)

    üîÑ "I dragged pages to the wrong spot"

    Just drag them again. Nothing is permanent until you hit download. You can rearrange as many times as you want.

    üì± "Drag and drop doesn't work on my phone"

    On phones, tap and hold the page thumbnail, then move it. If it's still not working, try using a different browser. I tested on Chrome and Safari and both work.

    üîí "It won't let me organize the pages"

    Password-protected PDFs can't be edited. Use the Unlock PDF tool first, then come back here to organize.

    üêå "The page thumbnails are loading slowly"

    Really big PDFs take time to generate previews. If you have a 200-page document, it might take a minute to show all the thumbnails. Be patient, or try organizing in smaller batches.

    üìâ "The quality looks worse after organizing"

    It shouldn't. The tool just reorders pages - it doesn't recompress them. If quality seems off, check if the original had issues. If it's a bug, email me.
