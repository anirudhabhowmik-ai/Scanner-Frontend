    Rotation vs. Flip: Understanding the Difference

    • Rotate: Turns the page clockwise or counter-clockwise (90°, 180°, 270°). Content is repositioned.
    • Flip/Mirror: Creates a mirror image of the page (horizontal or vertical flip).
    • Our Tool: Provides rotation only (90°, 180°, 270°). For flipping, use image editing software first.
