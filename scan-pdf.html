Why Use an Online Document Scanner?

Converting paper documents to digital format is essential for record keeping, sharing, and archiving. Our free online document scanner transforms your smartphone or computer into a powerful scanning tool. With automatic edge detection, perspective correction, OCR text extraction, and instant PDF conversion, you can digitize receipts, contracts, business cards, and any document in seconds — no app installation required.

✓ 100% Free & Secure: No registration required. No watermark added. All documents are processed in real-time and automatically deleted from our servers after scanning. Your privacy is our priority.

Key Scanning Features

  • Automatic Edge Detection: Our AI automatically detects document edges and suggests the perfect crop area.
  • Perspective Correction: Fixes skewed documents by applying geometric corrections for a flat, professional look.
  • Image Enhancement: Automatically adjusts brightness, contrast, and sharpness for optimal readability.
  • OCR Text Extraction: Extracts all text from scanned documents, making it searchable and copyable.

Tips for Best Scanning Results

  • Good Lighting: Scan documents in bright, even lighting to avoid shadows.
  • Flat Surface: Place documents on a flat, contrasting background for better edge detection.
  • Steady Camera: Keep your camera steady when capturing to avoid blur.
  • Full Frame: Ensure the entire document is visible within the camera frame.
  • High Resolution: Use the highest resolution camera setting available.
  • Remove Shadows: Avoid casting shadows on the document while photographing.

How OCR Technology Works

Our OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology analyzes the scanned image, identifies text characters, and converts them into editable, searchable text. This is especially useful for extracting information from receipts, business cards, contracts, and forms. The extracted text can be copied, edited, or saved as a text file for further use.
