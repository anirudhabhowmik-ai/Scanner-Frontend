    Split vs. Extract vs. Delete: Understanding the Differences

    • Split PDF: Divides a PDF into multiple separate files (each page becomes its own file, or selected pages become individual files).
    • Extract Pages: Creates a new PDF containing selected pages from the original (output is a single PDF, not multiple files).
    • Delete Pages: Removes pages from the original PDF and saves the remaining pages as a new file.
    • Our Tool: Splits PDFs into multiple files — perfect when you need each page as a separate document.
